# AiphaBee — Brand Brief

**Brand name:** AiphaBee (stylized "Ai" + "Bee")

**One-line positioning:** An AI investment-research agent for everyday investors — a tireless worker bee that digs up *alpha* across HK & US equities, turning institutional-grade research into one-tap insight.

**Core keywords (3–5):** AI Investment-Research Agent / HK & US Equity Research (港美股投研) / Stock & IPO Insight / Valuation & Risk Scoring / Retail Investor Insight Platform

**Target audience:** Primarily **C-end retail investors** trading **HK & US stocks (港美股)** — individuals who want clear, trustworthy, institutional-grade research and a second opinion before they buy, without the jargon, the terminal, or an analyst team. Skews mobile-first and time-poor; values a confident recommendation with the reasoning shown.

**Overall style direction:** Warm, trustworthy, intelligent fintech. Honey-and-navy palette with a diligent, bespectacled worker-bee character woven through the product. Clean, data-dense but friendly; signature honeycomb-hexagon geometry; bilingual (Simplified Chinese + English) voice. Confident and analyst-grade — states findings and gives a recommendation, always quantifying confidence and risk.

**Slogan:** "Find the alpha. Let the bee do the digging." (CN: 「采集 Alpha，交给勤劳的工蜂。」)

**Industry type:** FinTech · Investment research & capital markets (IPO / equity research / insight platform).

**Primary color tendency:** Honey Yellow `#FBCB0A` (primary — energy, optimism, the "honey"/alpha) + Deep Navy `#1A2242` (ink — credibility, trust). Functional accents: Growth Green `#10B981` (profit/bullish), AI Violet `#8B5CF6` (AI features), Startup Red `#EF4444` (risk/bearish). Light, clean surfaces; no heavy "AI" gradients.

**IP character + direction:** Yes — essential to the brand. A chubby, friendly **research bee** with round nerd glasses, honey-yellow + navy stripes and a bold navy outline, framed by a honeycomb hexagon. Personality: **diligent, hard-working, smart, helpful** ("worker bee" / 工蜂). The bee actively forages, researches, and presents findings — appearing in AI-insight blocks, loading/empty states, and onboarding. NOT baby-cute, NOT mascot-gimmicky; a credible, likeable analyst-bee.

**Flagship products / services:**
- AI stock-research agent for **HK & US equities** — ask anything, get a clear, sourced answer with a view.
- Multi-model valuation (DCF / comparables / precedent), risk scoring, and 6-dimension analysis, in plain language.
- IPO research — AI prospectus / S-1 analysis, market-sentiment & oversubscription (demand) signals, cornerstone & institution scoring.
- Watchlist insight, earnings & filing digests, and an insight platform / MCP feeding the research agent.

**Brand story / regional character:** A bee gathers honey the way a great analyst gathers alpha — tirelessly, cell by cell. The **honeycomb** stands for structured, collective intelligence; each hexagon a unit of verified research. Spanning **HK & US markets (港美股)**, AiphaBee puts a diligent research worker-bee in every retail investor's pocket — institutional rigor, made approachable.

**Preferred application directions:** Product UI (web app + dashboards), marketing site & landing pages, pitch/IR decks, research reports, mascot sticker/illustration set, social and onboarding. Bilingual zh/en throughout; numbers always in a tabular/mono face.

**Styles to avoid:** Generic "AI slop" — blue-purple gradients, emoji-cluttered cards, rounded-box-with-colored-left-border tropes. Cold Bloomberg-terminal density. Baby-cute / kawaii mascot. Dark "crypto-bro" neon aesthetic. Stocky corporate photography. Anything that could belong to any generic SaaS template — every surface should read unmistakably AiphaBee.
