# AiphaBee Terms of Service

Status: local publication draft requiring legal review.

## Research Not Advice

AiphaBee provides research tooling, data organization, deterministic analysis,
and evidence-linked explanations. The service does not provide trading,
portfolio management, personalized investment advice, or execution services.
It does not collect risk tolerance answers to produce automated suitability
conclusions.

## Data Rights

Market data, filings, analytics outputs, exports, and MCP/API access are subject
to dataset rights, package entitlements, channel restrictions, field-level
authorization, export limits, and partner commercial terms.

## Acceptable Use

Users must not bypass quota, scrape data beyond package rights, redistribute
data without authorization, misuse MCP/API access for prohibited commercial
distribution, upload unlawful content, or interfere with platform security.

## Service Changes

AiphaBee may change routes, tools, quotas, documentation, package entitlements,
or service availability. Breaking MCP changes require versioning and deprecation
notice according to the public tool-versioning contract.

## Current Publication Limits

This draft is not a final customer agreement. It requires legal, compliance,
commercial, and data-partner review before public launch.
