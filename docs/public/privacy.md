# AiphaBee Privacy Policy

Status: local publication draft requiring legal review.

## Data Minimization

AiphaBee should collect and process only the account, workspace, product usage,
research, support, and billing metadata needed to provide the service and comply
with security, audit, and retention obligations.

AiphaBee does not collect risk tolerance answers to produce automated
suitability conclusions.

## Authorized Memory

Session memory is limited to user-authorized preferences and consent records,
such as locale, response depth, default currency, workspace preference, data
retention acknowledgement, and MCP scope consent. Raw prompts, generated
answers, financial values, account credentials, and session credentials are not
valid authorized-memory payloads.

## Support Access

Support investigation should use visible `request_id` values, data versions,
tool names, error codes, and billing/usage references. Support surfaces should
not expose unnecessary user-sensitive content by default.

## Retention And Deletion

Account data export and deletion requests must follow the retention strategy,
audit requirements, legal hold requirements, and product safety requirements
defined by the account data lifecycle contracts.

## Current Publication Limits

This draft is not a final legal policy. It requires privacy, legal, security,
and vendor-risk review before public launch.
